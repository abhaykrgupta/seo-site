# The 50/30/20 Rule

Managing your money doesn't have to be complicated. The **50/30/20 rule** is a simple yet effective way to manage your budget.

## How it Works

1. **50% for Needs**: Housing, utilities, groceries, and insurance.
2. **30% for Wants**: Dining out, hobbies, and entertainment.
3. **20% for Savings**: Debt repayment and emergency funds.

> "A budget is telling your money where to go instead of wondering where it went." - John Maxwell

By following this rule, you can ensure that you are covering your essentials while still enjoying your life and building a secure financial future.
